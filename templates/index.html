Welcome to NETH4CK


NETH4CK is an advanced network penetration testing tool designed to streamline the initial phases of internal penetration testing. It simplifies the process for pentesters by automating the steps needed to infiltrate into internal networks.

Initially, NETH4CK assists by configuring a Raspberry Pi, which, once inserted into a target machine within the company, scans the internal network to identify an available IP address that auto-assigns it to the Raspberry Pi, integrating it into the network.

Once inside, NETH4CK leverages powerful enumeration techniques. It detects and responds to network queries, exploiting weaknesses in protocols like LLMNR, NBT-NS, and MDNS to intercept and capture critical information. NETH4CK can then impersonate services, redirect traffic, and capture sensitive data such as hashes and credentials, setting the stage for further penetration testing efforts.
